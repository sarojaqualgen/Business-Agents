QUALIFIED DOMESTIC RELATIONS ORDER
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This Court, having jurisdiction over the parties and subject matter,
hereby issues this Qualified Domestic Relations Order (QDRO) pursuant
to ERISA § 206(d) and IRC § 414(p):

1. PARTICIPANT: Amara Osei, SSN on file with plan administrator.

2. ALTERNATE PAYEE: Thomas Osei, 1847 W. Cermak Road, Chicago, IL 60608.

3. PLAN: Capital One Associate Savings Plan
   Plan Sponsor: Capital One Financial Corporation
   Plan Administrator: Aldergate Benefits Administration

4. BENEFIT ASSIGNED: 50% of the participant's vested account balance
   as of the date of segregation, including investment gains and losses
   attributable to the assigned portion from the date of this Order.

5. PAYMENT PERIOD: During the accumulation phase — benefits to be
   segregated into a separate account for the alternate payee and
   maintained until the alternate payee elects a distribution.

6. The alternate payee shall have all rights of a beneficiary under
   the plan with respect to the assigned portion.

7. This Order does not require the plan to provide any type or form
   of benefit, or any option, not otherwise provided under the plan.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IT IS SO ORDERED.
